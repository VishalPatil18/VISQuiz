<!-- HOW TO USE -->

## **🤔 How To Use**

Go to [this](https://visquiz-development.netlify.app/) site to play your favourite quiz. On the homepage you see a list of quizes. To start playing one just click on the quiz card. To check all the available quizes click on the **Find Quizes** or **See all Quizes** buttons.

<br />

<!-- KEY FEATURES -->

## **🎖 Key Features**

- Find Crazy quizes to challenge your friends
- Create your own Quizes
- Detailed Results and Analytics
- Search Quizes at your fingertips
- Login and Signup
- Short quiz which saves time and proves your worth with your sporting knowledge
